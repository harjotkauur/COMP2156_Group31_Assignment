Learning DevOps is crucial in the software development cycle as it enhances collaboration between development and operations teams, leading to faster and more reliable software delivery. DevOps engineers automate processes, ensure continuous integration, and streamline deployment, reducing errors and improving overall efficiency. Adopting DevOps practices accelerates development cycles, enhances product quality, and enables organizations to adapt swiftly to changing market demands, making it an indispensable skill in modern software development.
