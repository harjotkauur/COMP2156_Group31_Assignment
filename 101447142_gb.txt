George Brown College is a public college of applied arts and technology located in downtown Toronto, Ontario, Canada. It offers a blend of theory and hands-on learning, as well as applied research, entrepreneurship, and social innovation opportunities. The college provides over 170 full-time programs in various fields such as art and design, business, community services, early childhood education, construction and engineering technologies, health sciences, and hospitality and culinary arts. It also offers degree programs in conjunction with Toronto Metropolitan University. George Brown College aims to help students reach their career goals by providing a variety of program options, flexible scheduling, and industry partnerships that offer real-world experience and employment opportunities The college's three campuses are situated in the heart of downtown Toronto, allowing students to take advantage of the city's status as a major economic hub and gain an edge in the job market through its partnerships with companies and organizations.
